Mini-Twitter


Mini-Twitter allows user to add friends and add them in a private group.


User can check who their followers are and tweets among friends.

The user,Tiffany Young, has successfully been tweeted to her friends, Henry Ly and Thomas Seng.

Her friend, Thomas Seng, has received her tweet for the meeting plan on the weekend.

Thomas Seng has tweeted and agreed to meet up with her on the weekend.

Another friend of Tiffany, Henry Ly, has also agreed to join the meeting.

Mini-Twitter can sort positive messages/tweets and displays the total number of these positive messages to the user.

It can also count the number of users and the number of groups.

Lastly, it can also count the total number of tweets.
